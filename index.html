Common Questions

Do you lock creators into contracts?

No, we believe in earning your trust every single month. We never lock our creators into restrictive, long-term contracts. You always maintain complete freedom and flexibility over your career.

Do you post content without approval?

Absolutely not. You maintain 100% control over your image, your brand, and your boundaries. Nothing is ever posted to your accounts without your explicit, prior approval.

Do I need experience?

No prior experience is necessary to join our society. Whether you are starting entirely from scratch or already have a following, our expert team provides the step-by-step guidance and strategies you need to succeed.

Do I need expensive equipment?

Not at all. You can run your entire creator business from anywhere in the world using just your smartphone and a reliable internet connection. We will guide you on the best ways to shoot high-converting content easily.

How do payments work?

Your security is our priority. All platform earnings are deposited directly into your personal bank account first. We only invoice you for our agreed agency percentage at the end of the month after you have already been fully paid.
